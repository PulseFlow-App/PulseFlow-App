# Pulse Product

## What Pulse Does

Pulse is a data-first wellness platform designed to turn everyday lifestyle inputs into clear, practical guidance.

It connects food, movement, recovery, and body metrics into a **single daily signal** that helps people make better decisions without constant tracking or analysis.
