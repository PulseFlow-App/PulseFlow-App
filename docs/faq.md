### What can I log?

- Meals (text or photo, including fridge photos for meal ideas)
- Movement and workouts
- Sleep duration and quality
- Mood and energy
- Weight, body composition, smart scale data
- Optional: blood panels, body comp reports (for pattern exploration; non-medical use only)

### How does fridge-to-meals work?

You upload a photo of your fridge. Pulse detects ingredients, then suggests meals you can make with what you have, filtered by your goals (e.g. energy, time, recovery).
