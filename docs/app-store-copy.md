# App Store–Safe Copy

Use this language in the app and in store listings. No crypto, no locking, no token-gating.

---

## In-App (Profile / Premium)

**When user is not premium:**

- **Title:** Premium
- **Short description:** Unlock unlimited requests, long-term trends, and personalized insights.
- **Primary action:** Subscribe to Premium (opens IAP when implemented).
- **Secondary (optional):** “Learn about Pulse” → opens pulseflow.site or community link (no mention of token or locking).

**When user is premium:**

- “Premium active. Full features unlocked.”

**Do not use:**

- “Connect wallet to unlock premium”
- “Lock $PULSE to unlock”
- “Token holders get…”
- Any link that says “Buy token” or “Lock to unlock”

---

## Store Listing (Apple / Google)

**App name:** Pulse

**Short description (e.g. subtitle):**  
Daily wellness and routine pulse. Track body signals, check in, and get personalized insights.

**Full description (example):**  
Pulse helps you stay in tune with your body and routine. Log sleep, energy, mood, and more. Get a daily Pulse score and clear, actionable insights. Premium adds unlimited requests, long-term trend tracking, and advanced personalization. Subscribe inside the app to unlock everything.

**Keywords:**  
Wellness, routine, habits, body signals, daily score, insights, tracking.

**Do not include:**  
Crypto, token, locking, wallet, $PULSE, blockchain, in store listing or in-app purchase description.

---

## “About Pulse” / Community (Off-App Only)

On your **website** or **community** (not in app store copy):

- “Pulse is an app you can use every day. $PULSE is how the system evolves.”
- “You don’t need crypto to use Pulse. Crypto is for those who want to help shape it.”
- Link to governance, Pulse Lab, rewards — all clearly off-app.
